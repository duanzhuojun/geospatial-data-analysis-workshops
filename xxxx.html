Thunderstorm & Geospatial Data Mining Workshop

Exploring Data-Driven Approaches to Severe Weather Analysis

About the Workshop

This workshop introduces participants to data mining and geospatial analysis techniques applied to thunderstorm and severe weather studies. Topics include lightning data analysis, spatial clustering, and large-scale environmental datasets.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the structure of geospatial and meteorological datasets
  • Apply data mining techniques to thunderstorm-related data
  • Explore spatial and temporal patterns in lightning and weather events
  • Gain hands-on experience with reproducible data analysis workflows
